About

Pine City Mall is the ultimate fashion, entertainment and dining destination in Pretoria. With over 100+ stores, 20 eateries and a host of exciting entertainment, visitors are spoilt for choice! At Pine City you will find local South African retail, popular international brands such as Forever New, G-Star-Raw and everything in between. Montana Park also features a 5 cinema Ster Kinekor complex, IMAX Theatre. Prepare your senses for a world class experience at one of the largest shopping centres in the Southern Hemisphere.
